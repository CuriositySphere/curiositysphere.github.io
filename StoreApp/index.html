About Us

The school provides a structured learning environment that meets the unique needs of adult students as they work toward a high school diploma. Given students' range of skill levels and backgrounds, The Excel Center offers flexible coursework and a commitment to individual attention that gives each student a realistic path for his or her educational success and career prospects.

Madison Gwinn Reclaims Her Life at Excel Center

When Madison Gwinn started high school in Greenfield she was a cheerleader, participated in theater and earned good grades. However, by her junior year all that changed. She hung out with a rough crowd, stopped extracurricular activities and her grades plummeted. Eventually, Madison realized things were out of control and removed herself from the negative environment.

Madison heard good things about The Excel Center and along with two childhood friends enrolled. At The Excel Center Madison found her way again. She worked hard to improve her GPA and earned college credits in multiple classes.With her education on track, Madison got back into theater, recently auditioning for a starring role in Mary Poppins. She plans to study theater at Ball State or IU East this fall. She credits The Excel Center for helping her become a better version of herself.
